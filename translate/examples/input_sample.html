Chapter 1: The Awakening

Feng slowly opened his eyes, feeling a strange power coursing through his veins. The morning sunlight filtered through the ancient trees, casting dappled shadows on the forest floor.

"Where am I?" he whispered to himself, his voice barely audible over the rustling leaves.

Suddenly, a mechanical voice echoed in his mind: [System Alert: Host has awakened to the Absolute Finger technique]

Feng's heart raced. He had heard legends of the Absolute Finger, one of the most powerful techniques in the Spirit Bristle Nine Yang Divine Art. But those were just stories... weren't they?

He stood up, brushing off the dew from his robes. The Qi in the air felt different today—thicker, more vibrant. It was as if the world itself had changed overnight.

"System, show me my status," he commanded, half-expecting nothing to happen.

To his astonishment, a translucent blue screen materialized before his eyes:

Host: Feng

  • Cultivation: Qi Condensation Level 3
  • Techniques: Absolute Finger (Beginner)
  • Quest: Reach Qi Condensation Level 5 within 30 days
  • Reward: Unknown

Feng's breath caught. This was real. The System was real. And it had chosen him.

A rustling in the bushes behind him made him spin around. A figure emerged from the shadows—tall, imposing, with eyes that seemed to pierce through his very soul.

"So," the stranger said, his voice cold as ice, "you're the one who awakened the Absolute Finger. I've been waiting for this moment."

Feng's instincts screamed at him to run, but his feet remained rooted to the spot. Something told him that running would be futile.

"Who are you?" Feng demanded, trying to keep his voice steady.

The stranger smiled, but there was no warmth in it. "Someone who has been watching you for a very long time. Someone who knows what you're capable of... and what you will become."

Before Feng could respond, the stranger raised his hand. A dark energy coalesced around his palm, forming a swirling vortex of power that made the air itself seem to scream.

"Let's see if the legends are true," the stranger said, and attacked.
